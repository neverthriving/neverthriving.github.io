The Minnesota Neverthriving

nev·er·thriv·ing : noun. a flock of jugglers.

The Minnesota Neverthriving meets Monday nights from approximately 7:00 pm till 9:00 pm in the Matthews Park Building on 29th Ave & 24th Street in south Minneapolis. Bring your own props.
