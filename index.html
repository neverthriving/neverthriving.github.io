The Minnesota Neverthriving

nev·er·thriv·ing : noun. a flock of jugglers.

The Minnesota Neverthriving meets in the Matthews Park Building on 29th Ave & 24th Street in South Minneapolis on Mondays and Saturdays.

Mondays 6:00 pm until 8:00 pm. (Summer hours.)

Saturdays 2:00 pm until 4:00 pm.

Bring your own props.
